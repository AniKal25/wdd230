Target Audience and Scenarios

This section describes the target audience for the Global Clime website.

  • Who:The target audience are people (male and female) between 12-90 years who will easily access the website to check the weather information.
  • Age:The target audience are people (male and female) between 12-90 years, who are seeking any kind of information about the weather.
  • Income:$100,000 - $250,000 annually
  • Education: High School, university graduates and professionals.
  • Technology: Visitors can visit our site via mobile devices, laptop and tablets to access any information about the region and weather.
  • Motivation: Learn more about the weather and coordinate local events by knowing upcoming events. The public is aware of what is happening and can plan their activities appropriately.
