SITE PURPOSE

Weather forecasts are useful because they provide information that lets a person make a better decision than would otherwise be possible. The significance of weather in our daily lives is that we all experience it whatever form it takes. At Global Clime we know how important weather forecast is to most aspects of day to day life, including aviation, boating, and other modes of transportation, farming, tourism, sports, events and family gatherings. Without accurate weather forecasts people may end up in dangerous situations they were unprepared for and end up injured or worse.
